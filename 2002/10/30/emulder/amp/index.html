ABSTRACT: What’s New in J2EE 1.4?  (Web Services and more)

The new J2EE spec is almost out… which means that the time has come for all good developers to begin playing around with the latest features. Fortunately, integrated web services, schedulers, ‘codeless’ tag libraries and the new JSP expression language promise faster, more flexible development. In this session, we’ll take a look at important changes to EJBs, Servlets and JSPs, go over timelines for server support and discuss strategies for both new development and the migration of existing J2EE applications.

SPEAKER BIO:  Erin Mulder

Erin Mulder has been working with Java technology since its inception and is a J2EE architect with Chariot Solutions, a custom software development and systems integration company. In recent years, she has helped deliver many successful J2EE applications as well as speaking at the JavaOne developer conference and editing several Java and XML books. Erin also works to improve early technology education by speaking to students and working with schools and both the high school and elementary levels.
